About Us

Sony Marathi is a premium entertainment channel in Marathi from Sony Pictures Networks India.

The channel creates a unique space for itself in the Marathi general entertainment industry bycasing the glory of being Marathi and not only integrating the cultural ethos of Maharashtra but also catalysing cultural innovations. The content is curated by some of the best Marathi directors, writers and producers of the entertainment industry. Sony Marathi believes in staying connected to its viewers, all the time & The Selfie Jatra contest is one such endeavour. Subscribe, Participate, Stay Connected & Win with Sony Marathi.
